CH-47 Order Form

Versatility

The Chinook is capable of operating in all enviornments, from the hot and high conditions of the Afghan mountains, to the depths of Norway.

Lift

With 3 cargo hooks, the Chinook has the capability to carry underslung equipment up to 11300KG in weight. Anything from armoured vehicles to artillery, and even other aircraft.

Self-Defence

The Chinook is fitted with 1.5 tonnes worth of ballistic armour, coupled with 2x M134 Miniguns on either side of the aircraft with an M60 on the ramp.
